Privacy Policy for Bobl

Last Updated: 08/22/2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Bobl, a platform designed to help users create social outings within their community and invite others to join. Bobl is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We may collect personal details such as your name, email address, and phone number when you register for an account.
  • Location Data: Bobl uses your location to match you with relevant social outings and events. This includes real-time location data and the locations of venues you choose to attend.
  • Event Data: Information related to the events you create or attend, including date, location, and type of activity.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access the app, including your IP address, browser type, and operating system.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our app, such as the features you use and the pages you visit.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide Our Services: We use your personal information to create and manage your account, match you with relevant social outings, and facilitate communication between you and other users.
  • Personalization: Your location data and event preferences are used to suggest events and outings that match your interests.
  • Marketing and Advertising: We may use your information to display targeted ads, including push notifications about events and offers near your location.
  • Analytics: We analyze usage data to improve our services and develop new features.

4. Sharing Your Information

  • With Other Users: Certain information, such as your name and event participation, may be visible to other users.
  • With Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as payment processing and hosting services.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety or that of others.

5. Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

  • Access and Correction: You have the right to access and update your personal information.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of receiving marketing communications by following the unsubscribe link in our emails or adjusting your settings in the app.
  • Data Deletion: You may request the deletion of your account and personal data by contacting us.

7. Children’s Privacy

Bobl is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. Please review it periodically for any updates.
