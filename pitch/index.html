About Bobl

Bobl is a platform where users can create social outings within their community and invite other users to request or decline to join. In turn, the user can go through the list of ideal users for such outings and accept or deny the requested user of their choice.

Our Mission

At Bobl, we aim to help our users meet people of similar interests as theirs. We use location-based data of the user and the venue at which such outings are held to match them with suggested 'bobls', and with this premise, user can create their different circles of people they would like to have unique experiences with.

Problems We Solve

Consumer Gap

There is a lack of a proper platform that caters to the average out-going consumer, who enjoys doing different activities with people of similar interests.

Business Opportunity

There is a lack of a platform that caters to not only the consumer but also businesses, addressing their social interests based on the consumer's location.

Our Solutions

User Interface

We have developed an interface that allows users to create "bobls," which can be shared with people in their vicinity. Users can specify the date, location, and type of activity for each "bobl."

Business Integration

Businesses have the opportunity to advertise their exclusive offers to people in their vicinity, either directly through the app or via push notifications during their events.

Use Case Extension Breakdown

1. Hyper-Local Focus

  • Real-Time Event Discovery: Provide real-time updates on spontaneous events happening nearby.
  • Neighborhood-Level Targeting: Focus on very local events that are relevant to specific neighborhoods or communities.

2. Enhanced Personalization

  • AI-Based Recommendations: Use advanced AI to suggest events based on detailed user preferences and past behavior.
  • Customizable Notifications: Allow users to set highly specific notification preferences for event types and times.

3. Decentralized Hosting

  • Host Autonomy: Give hosts more control over their events, such as setting custom rules, managing entries, and creating exclusive experiences.
  • Peer Reviews for Hosts and Attendees: Implement a robust review system for both event hosts and attendees to ensure trust and safety.

4. Integrated Social Features

  • Networking Tools: Provide tools for attendees to network before, during, and after events, like chat groups, forums, or video introductions.
  • Shared Experiences: Enable users to share their event experiences through integrated multimedia posts (photos, videos, live streams).

5. Enhanced User Experience

  • Simplified Event Creation: Make the process of creating and managing events as intuitive and quick as possible.
  • Seamless Integration with Other Services: Integrate with other apps and services (e.g., calendars, messaging apps) to streamline user experience.

Valuation Methodology Breakdown

1. Revenue Multiple Method

  • Calculation: Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) × 15x multiple
  • Justification: SaaS/social platforms typically trade at 10-20x ARR. We use 15x as a mid-range conservative estimate
  • Industry Standard: Comparable to companies like Discord, Snapchat in early stages

2. User Value Method

  • Calculation: Total Users × ₦83,333 per user
  • Justification: Based on social platform acquisitions (Instagram: $77/user, WhatsApp: $55/user, adjusted for Nigerian market)
  • Conservative Approach: Uses lower-end valuations for emerging markets

3. Growth Premium Adjustment

  • Calculation: Average of above methods × 1.5 growth multiplier
  • Justification: Applied for high user growth rate (calculated daily from live data)
  • Risk Mitigation: Capped at ₦1.33B for seed-stage appropriateness

4. Final Valuation

  • Conservative Cap: Takes the lower of calculated value or ₦1.33B
  • Rationale: Prevents overvaluation typical in seed rounds
  • Equity Calculation: Funding Request ÷ (Valuation + Funding) = Equity %

5. Dynamic Data Validation

  • Live Metrics: Uses real user activity data, not projections
  • Time-Adjusted: Data period grows daily since launch (18/09/25)
  • Auditable: All calculations based on verifiable API data

This methodology follows standard venture capital and private equity valuation practices, making it defensible to investors and auditors.
