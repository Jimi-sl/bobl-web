Bobl Safety Standards Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

Effective Date: March 12, 2025
Last Updated: March 12, 2025

Bobl (https://webobl.com) is dedicated to providing a secure, respectful environment for all users. We take child safety extremely seriously and have zero tolerance for any form of child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or exploitation of minors.

1. Zero Tolerance Policy

  • We prohibit any content that depicts or advocates for the sexual exploitation or abuse of minors.
  • Any account found distributing or promoting such content will be subject to immediate suspension or removal.

2. Reporting and Removal

  • We encourage users to report suspicious or harmful content immediately by contacting our support team at [email protected].
  • Upon receiving a report, our moderation team will promptly review the content and remove it if it violates our policy.
  • If any content involving minors is discovered, we will take swift action, including reporting the matter to the appropriate law enforcement agencies where required by law.

3. Compliance with Law Enforcement

  • We cooperate with law enforcement agencies worldwide to combat child sexual abuse and exploitation.
  • Where legally obligated, we will share relevant information with authorities to assist in investigations related to child safety.

4. User Education and Awareness

  • We provide educational resources and guidelines to help users recognize, avoid, and report potential CSAE content.
  • Our Help Center includes articles and best practices on how to keep children safe online and how to report any concerning behavior.

5. Prevention Measures

  • We employ industry-standard measures (including content scanning, moderation workflows, and user behavior analysis) to detect and prevent the spread of CSAM.
  • Our systems are regularly reviewed and updated to stay ahead of emerging threats or tactics used by bad actors.

6. Accountability and Consequences

  • Users found violating these standards face immediate consequences, such as:
    • Account suspension or termination.
    • Reporting to law enforcement when applicable.
    • Removal of all offending content from our platform.

7. Policy Updates

  • We may update these Safety Standards from time to time to reflect evolving legal requirements or best practices.
  • Any updates will be posted at https://webobl.com/safety-standards.html. Your continued use of Bobl services signifies acceptance of any changes.
